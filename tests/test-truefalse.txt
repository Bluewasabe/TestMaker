## General Knowledge Quiz

1. The capital of France is Paris.
True
False
Answer: True

2. The Great Wall of China is visible from space with the naked eye.
True
False
Answer: False
Explanation: This is a common myth. The Great Wall is too narrow to be seen from orbit without aid.

3. Water boils at 100 degrees Celsius at sea level.
True
False
Answer: True
Explanation: At standard atmospheric pressure (1 atm / 101.325 kPa), water boils at exactly 100C.

4. Lightning never strikes the same place twice.
True
False
Answer: False
Explanation: Lightning frequently strikes the same location multiple times, especially tall structures.

5. The human body has 206 bones in adulthood.
True
False
Answer: True

## Science Questions

Q6. Does light travel faster than sound?
A. Yes
B. No
C. They travel at the same speed
D. It depends on the medium
Answer: A
Explanation: Light travels at roughly 300,000 km/s in a vacuum; sound travels about 343 m/s in air.

Q7. What is the chemical symbol for water?
A. W
B. Wa
C. H2O
D. HO2
Answer: C

Q8. Which planet is closest to the Sun?
A. Venus
B. Earth
C. Mars
D. Mercury
Answer: D
Explanation: Mercury is the innermost planet at about 57.9 million km from the Sun on average.
